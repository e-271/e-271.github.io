About Me
I am a second-year MS student at Virginia Tech working with Prof. Jia-Bin Huang. My primary research interests are in Reinforcement Learning and Data-Efficient Learning. In Summer 2020 I was fortunate to be a research intern at Google working with Sergio Guadaramma and Kuang-Huei Lee on inverse reinforcement learning, and in Summer 2019 working with Vincent Chu and Abhishek Kumar on GAN-based face generation. During undergraduate studies at Virginia Tech I worked with Prof. Mike Ruohoniemi and Prof. Joseph Baker on unsupervised radar data classification, and with Hongliang Xin on visualization of machine learning methods for catalyst discovery.
